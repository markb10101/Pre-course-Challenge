Hello, I'm Mark.

I grew up in the North West of England and gradually moved south before settling in Bristol about 10 years ago.

I started out working in Publishing before moving into Web in the late 90s. After 8 years, I left and pursued careers in music production and indie game develpment. More recently, for a change of pace, I had my own dogboarding business.

Now I have decided to return to Web Development and I'm currently re-training as a Front-end Web Developer through a course run by nology.

I'm particularly interested in the fields of VR, AR and AI.

When I'm not working, I enjoy spending time making music with my modular synthesiser, playing strategic board games, dogwalking or just relaxing with a good book.
